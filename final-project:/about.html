About Me

My background, my "why," and where I'm headed.

Who is Tate Allred?

I am a student at Brigham Young University with a relentless drive for growth and achievement. My journey has been defined by leadership—from serving as Student Body President in high school to leading initiatives in my local community.

International Experience

One of the most transformative experiences of my life was serving a 2-year mission in Chile. During this time, I became fluent in Spanish and developed a deep appreciation for diverse cultures. More importantly, it taught me the value of service, discipline, and effective communication—skills that I now apply daily in the world of sales.

Professional Vision

Currently, I am leveraging my competitive nature and people skills as a Solar Sales Closer. I find immense satisfaction in hitting targets and helping families transition to clean energy. Looking forward, I aim to combine my sales experience with my education to become a top-tier Financial Advisor, helping others achieve long-term financial freedom.
