The High-Performance Roadmap

Winning in sales requires a resilient mind and body. My daily protocol is built on the principle that how you start the day determines how you finish it.

  1. Physical Priming & Recovery
    • 7:00 AM: Immediate Cold Plunge to reset the nervous system.
    • High-intensity Weight Lifting and targeted cardio.
    • Evening Sauna sessions for recovery and mental clarity.
  2. Mental & Spiritual Foundation
    • Scripture Study to maintain values and perspective.
    • Reading: "The Greatest Salesman in the World" by Og Mandino.
    • Spanish Fluency maintenance from my mission in Chile.

Mental Resilience: "GOOD"

In sales and life, problems are inevitable. Jocko Willink's philosophy of responding to adversity with "Good" is the foundation of my professional mindset.

"Unexpected problems? Good. We have the opportunity to figure out a solution. Still breathing? Good. You still got some fight left in you." — Jocko Willink

Financial Vision: The Power of Compounding

As a future Financial Advisor, I emphasize the "Time Value of Money." Investing while young is the single greatest competitive advantage an individual can have. By letting compound interest work over decades, wealth is built through patience and discipline.
